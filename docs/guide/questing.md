# Questing

Quests come in various forms and can be found:

* Recorded in the HELP section of your Paperdoll with the item that started the quest
* In the QUEST gump, accessible by clicking on quest giver NPCs, or by clicking on your character and selecting VIEW QUEST LOG in the context menu

!!! note ""
	Some quests are not tracked at all, but are rewarded once items have been turned in to NPCs.
