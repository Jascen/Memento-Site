A Ruins & Riches variant

We respect our heritage, but we want to earn it! Memento focuses on establishing a power curve so Players can keep improving their characters.

Play solo or with friends

Memento is an immersive experience that is designed for a single player or a few friends.

Play anywhere

We prefer you join us on the Online server, but we are open source so you may also download and run it locally.

Casual audience

Casual players are our main audience. We've got partners and kids, we get it!

Powergamers welcome

While we do focus on Casual players, there's easily more than 50 hours of content to enjoy.

Endless replayability

The Avatar's Ascent (roguelite), Temptations system, and monster races allow each character to have a unique experience.

Forget macroing. Just play!

Skill gain rates are tiered and we've added more natural skill checks during gameplay.

Skill cap

Initial skill cap of 1,000 skill points. Additional points are possible through character race, special game modes, and quests.

All skills are valuable

Skills have been reworked to ensure each has a purpose.

Secondary skills for everyone!

Gathering and Crafting are Secondary skills; they do not count against your skill cap.

Enjoy gathering

Glistening Veins (mining) and Rich Trees (lumberjacking) harvesting systems promote actively gathering over botting.

Craft useful equipment

Crafting skills can greatly benefit early, mid, and late game progression.

Enjoy the journey!
