# Online Server Rules

!!! tip "Enjoy the journey"
	This server variant was updated to encourage exploration and playing. Simply playing the game should level your skills.

1. No Player Killing (PK) or unconsented PVP- do NOT attack other players, even if you're playing an evil/murderer type character.

1. Stealing from players __is NOT allowed__.

1. Do NOT grief other players. For example, do not steal monster kills or lumberjacking rich nodes.

1. Macros are allowed, but discouraged.

1. Resource gathering bots are allowed, but you are limited to __one bot per resource type__.

1. Multiple accounts are allowed, but please don't go overboard
	- Don't make multiple accounts for starter gold/items
	- Don't create multiple accounts to create a House Teleporter network just because you don't have Mark

1. Multi-clienting is acceptable, just don't ruin the fun of others
	- You may only use ONE CLIENT to** kill monsters**, additional instances to bot resources/macro/train are OK
